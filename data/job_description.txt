About the Role
Redrob AI is hiring a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to build production-grade AI systems that power intelligent candidate discovery and ranking for the Indian hiring market. You will design semantic retrieval pipelines, fine-tune and evaluate LLM-powered agents, and ship models that understand job fit beyond keyword matching.

Responsibilities

- Build embedding pipelines, vector search, and re-ranking systems using modern NLP and LLM tooling
- Own end-to-end ML lifecycle: data preprocessing, feature engineering, training, evaluation, and monitoring

- Implement RAG and agentic workflows for explainable shortlisting
- Productionize models with MLOps best practices (CI/CD, experiment tracking, model versioning)
- Mentor engineers on ML system design, evaluation methodology, and responsible AI
